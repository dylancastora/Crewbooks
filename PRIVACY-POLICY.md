# Privacy Policy

**CrewBooks**
Last updated: March 7, 2026

---

## 1. Overview

CrewBooks ("we," "us," or "our") is a free, open-source bookkeeping application designed for film industry freelancers. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

CrewBooks is built with a privacy-first architecture: **your financial data, invoices, images, and emails never pass through or are stored on CrewBooks servers.** All of that data lives entirely within your own Google account.

---

## 2. How CrewBooks Works

CrewBooks connects to your Google account via OAuth 2.0. When you use CrewBooks:

- Your bookkeeping and invoicing data is stored in **Google Sheets** in your own Google Drive.
- Images and attachments are stored in **your Google Drive**.
- Emails (such as invoices sent to clients) are sent via **your Gmail** account.

CrewBooks acts as an interface to your own Google Workspace. We do not have independent access to, copies of, or control over that data beyond what you authorize in a given session.

---

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Authentication Information

When you sign in with Google (OAuth 2.0), we receive:

- Your name and email address, as provided by Google
- An access token that allows CrewBooks to interact with your Google account on your behalf

We do not store passwords. Authentication is handled entirely by Google.

### 3.2 Website Analytics

The CrewBooks web application is hosted on Cloudflare. Cloudflare Web Analytics is enabled, which may collect:

- Pages visited and time spent
- General geographic region (country/region level)
- Browser type and device type
- Referring URL

Cloudflare Web Analytics is designed to be privacy-preserving and does not use cookies or fingerprinting to track individual users across sessions. You can review Cloudflare's privacy practices at [cloudflare.com/privacypolicy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

We may also use Google Analytics in the future. If and when that is enabled, this policy will be updated to reflect it, and any applicable opt-out mechanisms will be disclosed.

### 3.3 Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

- Financial records, invoices, or bookkeeping data
- Payment card or banking information
- Images or file attachments
- Email content
- Any data entered into CrewBooks beyond what is listed in Section 3.1 and 3.2

### 3.4 Limited Use of Google User Data

CrewBooks' access to and use of data received from Google APIs is limited to providing and improving the features of the Service as described in this policy. We do not use Google user data for any secondary purpose, including:

- Advertising or targeted marketing
- Training AI or machine learning models
- Building user profiles or databases
- Selling or transferring data to third parties for any purpose unrelated to operating CrewBooks

This use complies with the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including its Limited Use requirements.

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect to:

- Authenticate you and maintain your session
- Facilitate the connection between CrewBooks and your Google account
- Understand aggregate usage patterns to improve the application (analytics)

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your information for advertising.

## 6. Data Retention

Because CrewBooks does not store your bookkeeping or personal data, there is no CrewBooks-side data to delete when you stop using the app.

Your Google account data (Sheets, Drive files, Gmail history) is retained according to Google's policies and your own account settings. You remain in full control of that data at all times and can delete it directly from your Google account.

We may retain anonymized, aggregated analytics data (as described in Section 3.2) indefinitely, as it cannot be used to identify you.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the right to:

- **Access** — understand what information we hold about you
- **Correction** — request correction of inaccurate information
- **Deletion** — request deletion of your information held by CrewBooks
- **Portability** — your data lives in your own Google account and is fully portable at any time
- **Withdrawal of consent** — revoke CrewBooks' access to your Google account at any time via [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you may also have rights under GDPR, including the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

For California residents, you may have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in Section 10.

---

## 8. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect the limited data we do handle:

- All data transmitted between your browser and CrewBooks is encrypted in transit via HTTPS.
- OAuth 2.0 access tokens are stored securely, never logged, and never exposed to third parties.
- Security procedures are in place to protect the confidentiality of any Google user data that passes through the Service during a session.

Because your bookkeeping data lives in your Google account, its security is also governed by your Google account settings. We strongly encourage enabling two-factor authentication on your Google account.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

CrewBooks is intended for use by adults engaged in professional freelance work. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 16. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.
